Welcome to TUDuam’s documentation!#

TUDuam (TU Delft UAM) is a Python library which provides a framework for designing UAM vehicles. This repository is developed by the Urban Air Mobility department at the faculty of Aerospace Engineering from Delft University of Technology. This course also serves as an extension to the UAM course
